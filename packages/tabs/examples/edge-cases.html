Manual Activation (activate-on-focus="false")

Tab requires Enter or Space to activate. Arrow keys only move focus without changing the active panel.

Tab 1 - Focus moves with arrow keys, but panel doesn't change until you press Enter or Space.
Tab 2 - This is useful when tab content is expensive to render or fetch.
Tab 3 - The dashed border indicates focus without activation.
Tab 4 - Press Enter or Space to activate this tab.
